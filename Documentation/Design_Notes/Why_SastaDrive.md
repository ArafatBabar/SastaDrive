# Why SastaDrive?

SastaDrive was created as an engineering learning platform focused on motor control systems.

The objective was not to create a commercial product but to gain practical experience in:

- Power electronics
- Embedded systems
- Motor control
- PCB design
- Hardware documentation

While the design contains limitations and areas for improvement, it represents an important milestone in the development of future Mekatra motor control platforms.

Every design revision contributes valuable engineering knowledge and experience.
